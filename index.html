Welcome to the Cool people webring!

What is a webring ?.
Conallia made this webring, view my site here --> https://conallia.neocities.org
Cool people webring is an invite only webring filled with cool people. Once you become a member, you are allowed to invite anyone. This webring doesn't really have much of a theme, although I assume it will mostly be made up of personal websites.

If the ring is broken, please submit an error report on GitHub.

Add this widget to your page if you're part of the webring. Make sure to replace "YOUR-SLUG-HERE" with your slug. You may style it however you want. :)

How to add members

If you have a github account, ask me to add you as a contributor and follow the steps, otherwise ask me to do it.

  • Go to https://github.com/Conallia/Cool-people-webring/blob/main/_data/members.csv
  • Click the pencil icon
  • Add the member in the same format as the others (comma separated values).
  • Done!
